Next steps

When using this template, it is important to define your application architecture early to ensure scalability, maintainability, and long-term quality.

Application architecture considerations

A well-structured foundation will help you avoid technical debt and improve collaboration across teams. The following areas should be defined early in your project lifecycle:

  • 1 Framework choice (if any)
  • 2 Folder structure conventions
  • 3 Runtime configuration strategy
  • 4 Testing and quality gates
  • 5 Deployment environments (staging / production)

Why this matters

Early architectural decisions reduce complexity later in development and make your application easier to scale, test, and maintain. This is especially important in long-lived projects where multiple contributors are involved.
